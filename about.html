Old Drynie House

Set within 20 acres of Victorian parkland, the luxurious Old Drynie House Bed and Breakfast is situated on the Black Isle. We are rated 4 Stars by VisitScotland and boast a 9.6 “Exceptional” rating on Booking.com. We were also awarded “Luxury B&B of the Year” for Scotland by the Luxury Travel Guide in February 2016 and Bed and Breakfast of the Year (North of Scotland) at the Scottish Hospitality Awards 2018
